Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a new habit?

Tap the "Add" button, enter your habit name, set your daily goal (1-10 times), choose an icon and color, then tap "Create".

How do I mark a habit as complete?

Simply tap the habit on your main screen. The number will increase and your streak will update when you reach your daily goal.

What happens if I miss a day?

Your streak will reset to 0, but your total completions remain. Don't worry - just start building your streak again the next day.

Can I edit or delete habits?

Yes. Tap edit or delete buttons, when habit is unfolded.

Is my data backed up?

All your data is stored locally on your device. Make sure to regularly back up your device to avoid losing your progress.

Why can't I add more than [X] habits?

The app is designed to focus on building consistency with a manageable number of habits rather than overwhelming you with too many.

The app isn't working properly

Try restarting the app first. If the problem persists, please email us with details about what's happening and your device model.
