Privacy Policy

Thank you for using Streak - Habit Tracker (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our App. We believe in keeping things simple and protecting your privacy.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information from users of our App. Your habit data stays on your device. We don't ask for your name, email address, or any other personal details.

2. Data Storage

All your habit tracking data is stored locally on your device. We do not have access to your habits, streaks, or any information you enter into the App.

3. How We Use Your Information

Since we don't collect personal information, we don't use or process any personal data. The App works entirely offline without sending data anywhere.

4. Sharing Your Information

We don't share any information because we don't collect any. Your data never leaves your device.

5. Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. No external services have access to your data.

6. Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, your information is as secure as your device. We recommend keeping your device secure with appropriate locks and security measures.

7. Children's Privacy

Our App does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13. The App is safe for users of all ages.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted here. Continued use of the App after changes means you accept the updated policy.
